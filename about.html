About Us

As a company our mission is to provide personal safety products that are accessible, discrete and promote a sense of safety.

As part of our studies at the University of Liverpool we were asked to design a product that could have a positive psychological impact. We realised that we all shared a universal experience of feeling unsafe at one point in our lives.

This was the inspiration of our product, a personal safety alarm that attaches to a lanyard or watch with an easily accessible switch to activate the 130dB alarm that can be heard from up to half a mile away. Our aspiration for the uWatch is to act as a deterrent and to provide a sense of safety by quickly alerting and drawing people's attention even from a distance

Support for the company

The University of Liverpool and Santander Universities UK are supporting enterprise and entrepreneurship ideas developed by undergraduate and postgraduate students and recent graduates. We are honoured to announce that SafeU were chosen to receive an investment directly from the Enterprise Fund.

As part of our studies we were invited to take place in the young enterprise finals, in which we pitched our product and business model to a variety of industry professionals and a panel of judges.
